Providing high quality information and advice in pregnancy.

Allowing informed decision making, reducing maternal anxiety and working towards reducing the number of preventable congenital malformations and the unnecessary termination of otherwise wanted pregnancies.

Welcome to the Bumps Website

This sister website provides reliable, evidence-based, and accurate information about use of medicines in pregnancy, in the form of freely available patient information leaflets called 'Bumps' ('Best Use of Medicines in Pregnancy').

Reporting exposure in pregnancy

In order to provide the best possible information to inform an individual risk assessment for a pregnant woman and her baby following exposure to drugs or chemicals. For case specific advice please contact UKTIS on 0344 892 0909.

NON LIVE COVID-19 VACCINES

There are currently no proven fetal risks following gestational exposure to ‘non-live’ vaccines. It is therefore considered highly unlikely that such vaccines would be harmful if administered in pregnancy.

However, for some of these vaccines, information regarding the safety of administration in pregnancy is either limited or lacking.
